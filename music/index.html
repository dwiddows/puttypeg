Guitar Chords

Many friends have said at different times that they really need a sheet of guitar chords. Not a book of "1000 basic chords", though those exist and are very useful, but a reliable list of a few basic chords in each key that can fit on one or two sheets of paper that you can print out, put in your pocket, take on holiday, write on, lose, print out again, etc.

If this is what you're looking for, try this chord sheet. (I made this years ago on an old Acorn machine, and nowadays I only have a scan of the existing hardcopies, so this is a fairly big file (1.3MB).)

For each of the 12 notes / keys (C, C#, D, Eb, E, F, F#, G, G#, A, Bb, B) this gives the Major, Minor, 7th, Min 7th, 6th, 9th, Maj 7th, and Sus 4th chords. That's more than enough to fake most of the songs you'll ever be asked to play round a campfire.
